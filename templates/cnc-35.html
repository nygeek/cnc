This calculator honors the late George Stibitz of Bell Labs, creator in the 1930s of a complex number calculator that was also one of the first demonstrations of telecomputing.

It also honors the HP-35, the first electronic scientific calculator. The HP-35 was released commercially in 1972 and was sold until 1975. The keyboard layout and color scheme is designed to evoke the HP-35, and its function replicates the RPN operating model of the HP-35.
